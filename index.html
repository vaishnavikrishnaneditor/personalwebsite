About

Where craft
meets vision

An engineering graduate who chose the edit suite over the office — Vaishnavi Krishnan trained at the prestigious Film & Television Institute of India (FTII), Pune, specialising in editing. Her FTII graduation film Happy Birthday (dir. Medhpranav Powar) won the National Award for Best Film on Family Values.

Her professional journey spans assisting on landmark Bollywood and Gujarati projects — Neerja (Ram Madhvani), Mom (Ravi Udyawar), and The Illegal (Danish Renzu) — to her most recent work on Dhabkaaro (2026). Beyond the timeline, she is an active sketch artist and digital illustrator, with a visual language rooted in portraiture, travel, and the everyday meal.

Available for editing and illustration commissions via Nithos Studios.
